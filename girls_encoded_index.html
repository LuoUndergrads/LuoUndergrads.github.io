Girls Encoded is a loose collaboration of faculty and students at the University of Michigan who are trying to encourage women to pursue computer science. Girls Encoded sponsors events for women of all ages, from elementary school through adults, to introduce women to the exciting world of technology and equip them with the tools they need to succeed in a computational field.
