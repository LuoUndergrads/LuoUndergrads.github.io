Girls Encoded is a program to develop the pipeline of women in computer science as well as to retain the women who have entered into the program. Run through the Computer Science Engineering (CSE) department at the University of Michigan within the College of Engineering, the program will have two focus areas: recruitment of women into the CSE department, and retention of women to graduation from department.

See our "Get Involved" page for ways that your company can support this program.
