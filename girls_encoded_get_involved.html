Companies

Are you a company interested in supporting women in computer science? We would love for you to partner with us and help us to inspire and encourage more women in technology. There are many ways that you can be involved, from sponsoring a specific event to sending students to conferences. You can choose to target your sponsorship toward recruitment or retention (or do both, if you prefer). Here are details about our pre-defined levels of sponsorship:

Gold Sponsor ($25,000)

Sponsorship Benefits with a Retention Focus:

  • Naming a “Women in Computing” seminar

  • Naming a fellowship to be awarded to three CS students to attend Grace Hopper, the largest gathering of women in technology in the world

  • Hosting an on-campus activity/event with the Girls Encoded community

Sponsorship Benefits with a Recruitment Focus:

  • Naming two computer science courses held at high-schools in Michigan

  • Naming a fellowship to be awarded to three CS students to attend Grace Hopper

Silver Sponsor ($10,000)

Sponsorship Benefits with a Retention Focus:

  • Naming a fellowship to be awarded to three CS students to attend Grace Hopper, the largest gathering of women in technology in the world

  • Hosting an on-campus activity/event with the Girls Encoded community

OR

  • Naming a fellowship to be awarded to six CS students to attend Grace Hopper

Sponsorship Benefits with a Recruitment Focus:

  • Naming one computer science course held at a high-school in Michigan

Bronze Sponsor ($5,000)

Sponsorship Benefits:

  • Naming a fellowship to be awarded to three CS students to attend Grace Hopper, the largest gathering of women in technology in the world

We are interested in working with you to find an exciting opportunity for you to be involved in the CS community here at U of M. Please get in touch with us at girls-encoded@umich.edu to discuss sponsorship or to ask any questions that you might have.
